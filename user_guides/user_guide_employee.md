2. Employee Guide

  This guide is for staff members using the system to track their work schedule and manage time-off requests.

  1. Your Dashboard (/dashboard)

  The dashboard is your primary source of truth for your work schedule.

  - Upcoming Shift Card: The top card always shows your very next assignment, including the duty name, date, and exact timings.  
  - Viewing Your Schedule:
    - Calendar View: A visual monthly grid.
        - Duty (Blue/Primary): A scheduled work shift.
      - WO (Grey): Weekly Off.
      - Today: Marked with a primary color circle.
    - List View: A detailed table showing Sign-On/Off times, locations, and total duration for every day of the month.
  - Understanding the Details:
    - Hover/Tooltips: In the calendar view, hover over a duty to see the full route (Start Location $\rightarrow$ End Location)  
  and the exact timing.
    - Update Badge: Look for the "Roster updated on..." badge at the top. If the date/time is recent, check your schedule for    
  changes.

  2. Leave & Duty Management (/employee-requests)

  Use this page to ask for time off or request a shift change.

  - How to Submit a New Request:
    a. Click the "New Request" button.
    b. Select Request Type:
        - Leave: If you need a day off.
      - Shift Change: If you want to swap your current duty for a different one.
    c. Set the Date: Choose the specific date you are requesting for.
    d. Select the Option:
        - For Leave, choose the specific type of leave.
      - For Shift Change, select the target duty you wish to be assigned to.
    e. Provide a Reason: Explain why you are making the request in the text box.
  - Tracking Your Request:
    - Your requests appear as cards.
    - Amber/Clock: Pending review by the planner.
    - Emerald/Check: Approved.
    - Rose/X: Rejected.
    - Planner Comments: If a request is approved or rejected, read the "Planner Comment" section to see the official feedback.
